Privacy Policy for Ai CV Builder

Effective Date: 24 February 2026

InfinityMmedia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (the "Ai CV Builder" or "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

Our goal is to provide a powerful CV building experience while respecting your privacy. We collect very limited data to enable core features.

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information (Email): To enable cloud syncing, account recovery, and the coin-based system across devices, we collect and store your email address when you sign up or log in. This is the only piece of personal authentication data we store on our servers.
  • CV Content: All personal data you type into your resumes (name, work experience, etc.) is primarily saved locally on your device. However, if you are signed in, this data may be synced to your private account to allow you to continue work on other devices.

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you access the App, certain third-party services we use may automatically collect non-personal device information:

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous interaction events to help us improve the App's UI/UX.

C. Third-Party Data Collection & Integrations

Our application integrates with third-party software development kits (SDKs) to provide functionality and display advertisements:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements via Google AdMob on mobile devices. AdMob may collect and process certain device data and ad interaction data to provide relevant ads. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy regarding personalized advertising.
  • Google Gemini & OpenAI: If you use our AI features (e.g., PDF parsing, text translation, or cover letter generation), the specific text you submit is sent securely to these APIs for processing. This data is transmitted statelessly via secure Edge Functions. We do not store this data, nor is your data used by OpenAI or Google to train their foundation AI models.

2. How We Use the Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Using your email to verify your identity and manage your account.
  • Feature Delivery: To operate and maintain the App's core functionality, including AI resume generation.
  • Monetization: Deliver targeted advertising via AdMob to keep the standard App features free.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. Data is only shared with service providers (like Google/OpenAI) strictly for the fulfillment of the user's request and is governed by strict enterprise data privacy rules.

4. Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has inadvertently provided personal information to third-party SDKs in our app, please contact us.

5. Your Data Rights & Deletion

You have complete control over your data and account.

  • In-App Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Inside the App, go to Settings -> Security & Data and press "Delete Account & Data". This will immediately and permanently purge your email, coin balance, and synced documents from our servers and your local device.
  • Local Deletion: You can also clear your data locally by clearing your browser/app cache.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon the posting of the revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
